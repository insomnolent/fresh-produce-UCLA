    • About us

    Fresh Productions (often known as "Fresh Produce") is a UCLA art club for artists of all levels! We offer workshops, drawing lessons, special art supplies, and more. Whether you're a traditional or digital artist, come join us to hang out, draw together, and make friends in a chill environment!

    For the Spring quarter, weekly meetings are on Tuesdays 8:00-10:00pm at the Ackerman Jamba Juice Lounge (unless stated otherwise)! Don't worry if you've missed a few meetings; all are welcome to come anytime.
